Hello everyone, I am Amartya Basu

Currently I am a MS(Research) scholar in Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Madras.

I am broadly interested in the application of IOT and wireless technologies that interact with and interpret (sense) the physical world, spanning both algorithm design as well as end-to-end system prototyping. I am guided by Dr Ayon Chakraborty. I am also a member of Sensing and Networked Systems Engineering(SENSE) Group at IIT Madras.

Before joining IIT Madras I was working as Assistant System Engineer at Tata Condultancy Services, Kolkata. My area of work was on the maintenance and upgradation of reverse proxy servers. I was also a member of the automation team in the project.

I have completed my undergraduation in Computer Science and Engineering from Government College of Engineering and Leather Technology, Kolkata. I was guided by Mr Debayan Ganguly in my Final Year Thesis. Our work was based on the application of sentiment analysis.
